Our Signature Dishes

Masala Dosa: A crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with spiced potatoes, served with coconut chutney and sambar.
Hyderabadi Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with tender chicken or vegetables, infused with aromatic spices.
Filter Coffee: Traditional South Indian coffee brewed with freshly ground coffee beans and served in a stainless steel tumbler.
